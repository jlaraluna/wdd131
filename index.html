Yucatan, Mexico

The Yucatán Peninsula is a region in southeastern Mexico known for its rich Mayan heritage, tropical climate, and stunning natural landscapes. It includes the states of Yucatán, Campeche, and Quintana Roo. The peninsula is home to ancient ruins like Chichén Itzá and beautiful cenotes, natural freshwater sinkholes found throughout the region.
