Calakmul Sanctuary

A UNESCO World Heritage Site nestled in the lush jungle of Campeche, Mexico.

Welcome to the Calakmul Sanctuary a place where sacred heritage meets ecological wonder.
Calakmul is a sanctuary that preserves the jungle’s biodiversity and the legacy of the ancient Mayan civilization. As part of the Biosphere Reserve, it invites responsible tourism through cultural, environmental, and historical awareness—deepening your connection with the land and its stories.

What can I expect to see and experience when visiting Calakmul?

Visitors want to know about wildlife, landscapes, historical ruins, and local culture before planning their trip.

How can I prepare for a responsible and environmentally conscious visit?

Many users seek guidance on eco-friendly travel practices, rules of preservation, and respectful interaction with nature and heritage sites.
