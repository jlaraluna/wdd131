Calakmul Sanctuary

The soul of the Mayan jungle

Site Name

Calakmul Sanctuary

The name Calakmul Sanctuary reflects the sacred and ecological significance of the Calakmul Biosphere Reserve. “Sanctuary” conveys a sense of protection, reverence, and refuge, emphasizing both the rich biodiversity of the jungle and the deep-rooted cultural history of this ancient Mayan site.

Optional domain availability: calakmulsanctuary.org

Site Purpose

The site introduces the Calakmul Biosphere Reserve and encourages responsible tourism through cultural, environmental, and historical insights.

Scenarios

  • What can I expect to see and experience when visiting Calakmul?
    Visitors want to know about wildlife, landscapes, historical ruins, and local culture before planning their trip.
  • How can I prepare for a responsible and environmentally conscious visit to the reserve?
    Many users seek guidance on eco-friendly travel practices, rules of preservation, and respectful interaction with nature and heritage sites.

Color Schema

The color schema of this site is designed to provide a clean, elegant visual experience. Below are the selected colors and their intended uses:

Primary Green (#2A3F32): Used for the header and footer background, giving the site a natural, grounded tone.

Secondary Orange (#A35C28): Applied to hover effects on links and buttons to create visual emphasis and interaction.

Light Calcite (#DAD2BA): Used as the main body background to keep the site soft and easy on the eyes.

Orange Accent (#C6844C): Adds warmth and contrast in small detail elements.

Text Dark (#041c20): Used for all main text content such as headings and paragraphs for optimal readability.

Background Default (#FFFFFF): Provides a clean backdrop for various content sections where necessary.

Typography

The typography strategy supports both elegance and legibility, balancing classic serif fonts with a modern UI font. The following fonts are used:

Cormorant Garamond (serif): Applied to all heading elements (h1–h6) to create a refined, high-end aesthetic.

Crimson Text (serif): Used for body text, paragraphs, and list items to maintain a literary, elegant tone.

Fira Sans (sans-serif): Designated for interface elements such as navigation, buttons, labels, small text, and form inputs to ensure clarity and usability.
